About me

This Portfolio Website discusses a brief personal biography and achievement. My name is Daniel Thongbor. I was born in Juba, South Sudan in Africa. Over twenty years ago, I came to the United States from Kakuma Refugees Camp in Kenya as one of the Lost Boys of Sudan. I was truely humbled and most appeciative to both the United States government and the Catholic Social Services for bringing my colleagues and I to this great country. I am now resided in Charlotte, North Carolina (NC).

After we arrived to Charlotte, NC, we started the processes of getting immigration documents, finding jobs, and enrolling in the community colleges and universities. I received my US citizenship in 2007 and acquired some school documents. These school documents encompass Science, International Studies, Cloud Computing Architecture, and current Full Stack Programming.
